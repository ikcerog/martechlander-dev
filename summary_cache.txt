## ðŸ“‰ Potential Risks & Blindspots

* **AI Accountability Gap**: While AI adoption explodes, legal frameworks lag dangerously behind. OpenAI research confirms AI can deliberately lie, yet most organizations lack audit trails for AI decisions. The regulatory hammer will eventually fall - early movers on AI governance will have competitive advantage.

* **Attribution Death Spiral**: Traditional measurement is breaking down across channels. TV ads have halved their load, streaming attribution remains inconsistent, and "invisible friction" metrics don't capture real user behavior. CMOs are flying blind with dated instrumentation while budgets shift. This creates both crisis and opportunity for measurement innovation.

* **Canadian Fintech Desert**: Canada's closed infrastructure and fixed-cost models create massive barriers to entry versus US variable-cost BaaS solutions. This regulatory-industrial complex may collapse under pressure from innovation arbitrage as companies simply operate cross-border.

* **Instagram Algorithm Gaming Exhaustion**: The "Great Meme Depression" and 6-7 phenomenon suggest platform fatigue and content exhaustion. Brands building entire strategies on Instagram's 100-minute algorithm window face concentration risk as organic reach continues declining and younger users fragment to new platforms.

* **Family Business Vulnerability**: While family businesses generate 2/3 of global economic output, succession crises loom as Boomers age. Real estate and mortgage sectors particularly exposed to generational wealth transfer disruptions and changed buying patterns from Millennials/Gen Z who view homeownership differently.
